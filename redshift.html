ABOUT

The Band

We Are Redshift

Hi! We are REDSHIFT, a young band from Guernsey. We love to put a fun spin on great classics and play them in our own way.

We started as a simple youth band playing local gigs but we are now looking to expand and reach new heights with recording of our best covers and hopefully soon originals!

We hope people enjoy out music as much as we love to play it.
